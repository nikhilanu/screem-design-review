The people list, twenty ways

screem · employees + candidates · same 67 people, different ideologies

Every variation lists the same 61 employees and 6 candidates and must answer: who needs me? (Maria, Jakub, Sam), is everyone else fine?, and how do I find anyone? — while keeping current staff and incoming candidates clearly distinct. Click any card to open it full size; click any person inside to reach the detail-page gallery.
