The home page, twenty ways

screem · v1.1 design system · same data, different ideologies

Every variation answers the same three questions — am I safe? (the verdict), what do I do? (Maria, Jakub, Sam), and is the rest handled? — but each stakes out a different idea of what a compliance home should be. Click any card to open it full size.

 02The letter

 03Agenda

A day-planner timeline — today, next week, August, autumn.

 04Checklist
